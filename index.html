About The Conference

Welcome to NCNTAIA - 2025

In an age of accelerating digital transformation, Artificial Intelligence (AI), Big Data Analytics, and Smart Computing are reshaping the global landscape across academics, industries, governance, and everyday life. The National Conference on Next-Generation Technologies in AI applications (NCNTAIA) is an important initiative to facilitate the exchange of cutting-edge research, breakthrough innovations, and impactful practices that define this new technological epoch.

This academic and industry-oriented conference seeks to bring together a diverse community of scholars, scientists, developers, educators, entrepreneurs, and policymakers to explore advancements that are fueling next-generation systems. With a strong focus on interdisciplinary collaboration, the event would provide a dynamic forum for the presentation of high-quality research results, the demonstration of real-world applications, and the discussion of strategies for responsible and scalable tech integration.

Call for Papers

Submission Guidelines

We invite researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to submit original, unpublished research papers on topics related to Artificial Intelligence and its applications. All submissions will be peer-reviewed by an expert committee.

Important Information

  • Papers should be in IEEE format (max 6 pages)
  • Submissions must be in PDF format
  • All papers will undergo plagiarism check
  • Accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings
  • Selected papers may be considered for publication in Scopus-indexed journals

Conference Objectives

Promote Research and Innovation

To provide a dynamic platform for sharing innovative research, emerging trends, and practical applications in Artificial Intelligence, Big Data Analytics, and Computer Applications.

Foster Collaboration

To encourage collaboration among researchers, industry experts, and academicians to present cutting-edge research and discuss emerging trends in AI, Big Data, and Smart Computing Applications.

Promote Sustainable Solutions

To address how AI technologies can be leveraged for sustainable development, solving challenges such as climate change, public health, and economic inequality.

Address National Challenges

Bring global perspectives to discuss national challenges and opportunities using AI and big data for innovation, national policy, and technological equity.
